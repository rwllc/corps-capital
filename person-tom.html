What Tom Approves

Almost everything routes through Tom for final sign-off. These are the specific approvals that require him:

  • Wire transfers (with Tino as 2nd approver)
  • Payroll (automated, Tom sets the direction)
  • Bill pay & DocuSign
  • Investment strategy decisions
  • New client relationships (big moments with Brian)
  • Bookkeeping and legal matters (through Bonnie)
  • All operational functions (through Tino)
  • P&L review and oversight
The principle: Tom lives in the lanes where he's irreplaceable — vision, marquee relationships, and final approval. Everything operational has a single, clear owner so Tom is never the bottleneck on execution.
